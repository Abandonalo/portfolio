ABOUT

01 / The Drive

Film taught me where to point attention. Computer science taught me how to build the experience around it.

I take intimidating things, AI tools, 3D scenes, research prototypes, and shape them into experiences where complexity feels manageable and the next step feels obvious. Research, design, and code, all in me.

I'm drawn to problems that fall between disciplines, where ownership is unclear and the solution has yet to take shape. I usually build to understand, test, and uncover what works. And I still get a genuine kick out of an interface that simply clicks.

02 / The Off-Screen

Off the clock, I'm usually outside with a nine-month-old puppy in training to become a service dog. It's a lot like design: small, repeatable steps, real patience, and the satisfaction of seeing it add up to something meaningful.

Mostly, I just enjoy making things. My current project is our wedding, which I'm designing from end to end: from the food and visual identity to the music and atmosphere.

Home is my partner, the pup, and whatever we're building or traveling toward next.
